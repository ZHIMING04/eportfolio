# Technology & Information System — UTM EDU

## 📘 Course Overview

**Technology & Information System (SECP1513)** introduced the fundamentals of information systems and information technology.  
This course covered computer hardware, software, networks, databases, data analytics, privacy, security, ethics, cloud computing, PC assembly, industry exposure, and career pathways in IT.

It helped me understand how technology supports real organizations and how IT solutions can be designed around user needs.

## 🛠 Coursework: UTM EDU Design Thinking Project

For the group project, we applied the **Design Thinking process** to propose an educational software solution called **UTM EDU**.

The project started with interviews to understand problems faced by UTM students and lecturers. From the findings, we identified several issues:

- Unclear event details
- Difficulty booking sports facilities
- Lack of real-time bus information
- Unclear cafe operating hours
- Hard-to-access university services
- Slow feedback channels

To solve these problems, we designed a low-fidelity prototype of **UTM EDU**, a centralized student app that includes:

- Event information and registration
- Sports facility booking
- Bus schedule and transport notification
- Cafe and restaurant information
- Important date reminders
- Feedback and survey feature

My contribution involved the interview, discussion, prototype, testing sections, video editing, progress tracking, final review, and feedback.
