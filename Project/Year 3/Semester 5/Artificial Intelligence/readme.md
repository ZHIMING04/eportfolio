## 🛠️ Coursework

### 🌊 Project Proposal: AI-Based Flood Management System

For the group project, my team proposed a **Smart Urban Application** under the theme of **Smart City**.

The project focuses on solving flood-related issues in Malaysia, where traditional flood management often depends on manual monitoring, delayed reporting, and slow response.

Our proposed solution is an **AI-based flood management system** that uses:

* Machine learning
* IoT sensor data
* Real-time environmental monitoring
* Historical flood records
* Rainfall and river-level data
* GIS-based dashboard visualization
* Early warning notifications

The system aims to predict flood risks earlier, alert residents and authorities, and support faster emergency response.

| Goal                 | Description                                                               |

| Flood Prediction     | Predict potential flood areas, severity, and timing                       |
| Early Warning        | Send alerts through dashboard, mobile notification, and SMS               |
| Real-Time Monitoring | Collect rainfall, humidity, river level, and water depth data             |
| Decision Support     | Help city planners and emergency teams respond faster                     |
| Long-Term Planning   | Support better drainage, infrastructure, and disaster prevention planning |

---

## 🧠 Design Thinking Process

### Empathize

We identified key users affected by flooding:

* Residents in flood-prone areas
* City planners
* Emergency response teams

Their main pain points included late flood warnings, poor communication, unclear risk areas, property damage, and anxiety during heavy rain.

### Define

From the user analysis, we defined the main needs:

* Accurate and timely flood alerts
* Clear location-based risk information
* Better coordination between agencies and residents
* Greater trust in flood prediction systems

## 🧠 Reflection

This course helped me understand how AI can be applied beyond theory to solve real community problems.

Through the flood management project, I learned how AI, IoT, real-time data, and predictive modelling can work together to support disaster prevention and smart city development.

Overall, Artificial Intelligence strengthened my ability to think about intelligent systems from both technical and social-impact perspectives.
