## 🧠 Industry Talk Reflection: EY

### Career Growth, Technology Consulting & Resume Building

I had the opportunity to attend the **EY Campus Tour and Industry Talk**, where I gained valuable insights into **technology consulting, career preparation, and professional growth**.

One key takeaway from the Technology Consulting session was that creating impact in a company is not only about joining a well-known organization. It is about building strong technical expertise, applying it to real client problems, and communicating solutions clearly so that clients can trust the work delivered.

The career preparation session was also very useful. Through the CV workshop and personalized advice from the EY HR team, I learned how important it is to present my technical projects in a clear and professional way. It helped me understand that a good resume should not only list skills, but also show results, ownership, and the value created through each project.

Another meaningful insight was EY’s emphasis on mentorship and teamwork, especially through their new-joiner buddy system. This showed me that professional growth is supported not only by individual effort, but also by guidance, collaboration, and learning from people around us.

Overall, the EY industry talk helped me better understand what consulting firms look for in young talent and motivated me to continue improving my technical skills, communication ability, and career readiness.

---

### 💡 Key Takeaways

* High-impact work comes from mastering useful skills and applying them to real problems.
* Technical projects should be presented with clear outcomes and value.
* Communication is important for building client confidence.
* Mentorship and teamwork are important parts of professional growth.
* Career preparation requires both technical strength and personal branding.
