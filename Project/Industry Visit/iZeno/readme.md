## 🧠 Industry Talk Reflection: iZeno

### Learning Beyond the Classroom with iZeno

I had the opportunity to attend an industry sharing session by **iZeno**, where I gained valuable insights into the real-world application of **Data, AI, and technology solutions**.

The session helped me understand that technical skills alone are not enough in today’s industry. Besides knowing tools and programming concepts, a strong technology professional also needs **continuous learning, adaptability, communication, and problem-solving skills**.

One key takeaway for me was that Data and AI are not only about building models or dashboards. They are about solving real business problems, supporting better decisions, and creating practical value for users and organizations.

As a **Data Engineering student**, this talk gave me a clearer picture of industry expectations. It motivated me to continue improving both my technical foundation and professional skills so that I can become more prepared for future internships, projects, and real-world data roles.

Overall, the iZeno industry talk was a meaningful learning experience because it connected classroom knowledge with actual industry practice.

---

### 💡 Key Takeaways

* Technical skills must be supported by strong communication and problem-solving skills.
* Continuous learning is important in the fast-changing field of Data and AI.
* Real-world technology solutions must focus on business value, not only technical implementation.
* Adaptability is important for working in modern IT and data-driven environments.
* Industry exposure helps students understand what companies expect from future talent.
